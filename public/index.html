TanGram

About TanGram

TanGram tells the story of Tan, a statue from TeenyTown, who has been swept away by the evil pigeons. Now Tan must power up, change form, get home, and stop the pigeons.

Inspired by classical 2D platformers, TanGram includes unique elements requiring Tan to constantly reinvent themselves and the player to use their imagination.

TanGram was developped by Heather Jang and Keith Reynolds. It was built using the Phaser library.
